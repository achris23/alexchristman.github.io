    • This page is dedicated to the top five places I have travelled to. A short description of each place I have been is included on this page. Pictures and informational links are located on the right hand side of the page (only viewable on desktop screen).

    Costa Rica

    Costa Rica is an amazingly cool place! While I was there I stayed in the Nicoya peninsula and around the Arenal volcano.

    San Francisco

    I visited San Francisco about five years ago and had an amazing time. I got to see the Golden Gate bridge and Alcatraz Island!

    Arenal Volcano in Costa Rica

    San Francisco

    Cancun

    Cancun is a really great place to visit. I had always wanted to go to a beach with clear blue water and Cancun did not disappoint.

    Charleston South Carolina

    My oldest sister, Shannon, lives in Charleston so its a place I visit quite often. Charleston is an old city with some amazing history. My favorite things about Charleston are the architecture of the buildings and their amazing food.

    Cancun Beaches

    Fort Sumter

    Savannah Georgia

    Savannah is only a couple hours away from Charleston and its another city I really love because of its history. One of my favorite parts about Savannah are all the parks located throughout the city. Just about every other block has a small park right next to it.
